What to have ready before you even think of applying to a job

This space is intended to help you get ready to look for a job at the same time you are studing hard in university.

University gets you started in different ways, It is really up to you to keep digging and learning. So here I will try to show you a few things you should have ready before you even think to apply.

  1. Create a LinkedIn account
  2. Create a git repository account either with Github or Bitbucket or Gitlab and start uploading your projects
  3. Start thinking of your resume
  4. Seek ways to gain experience (i.e. volunteering in foundations)
  5. What do you like? Do you know the field?.
  6. Pretend you are looking for a job in your area
  7. Gather in a paper all or most of the requirements that companies expect from their candidates and start learning

Learn how to search online

First of all, try to ask people around you, use Google and Youtube. I know there are a lot more, but these should give you enough idea of where to start.

The information is out there on the internet. But be careful! there is also incorrect information. Be smart on your search!

One of the skills you have to develop is to "Learn how to search propperly" or as I call it to "Learn how to ask the right question".
